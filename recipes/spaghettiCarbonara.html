Spaghetti Carbonara

This classic Italian recipe features a rich, creamy sauce made with eggs, Parmesan cheese, and guanciale (cured pork cheek). The simplicity of the ingredients belies the depth of flavor that can be achieved with this dish. With just a few key steps, you can enjoy a delicious and satisfying meal right at home.

Ingredients:

  • 500g of spaghetti;
  • 2 large eggs;
  • 50g of grated Permesan cheese;
  • 150g of guanciale or pancetta, cubed;
  • 1/4tbs of black pepper;
  • Olive oil;
  • 1 clove of garlic (optional);
  • Salt to taste.

Steps:

  1. Cook the pasta in salted water according to the packaging;
  2. Cook the guanciale or pancetta until crispy in a skillet over medium heat. If desired, add a clove of garlic for a few seconds & remove it;
  3. Prepare the sauce in a large bowl by whisking in the eggs, Parmesan cheese & black pepper;
  4. Combine the spaghetti and sauce. Toss around to coat the spaghetti properly.

TIP: Serve immediatelly with freshly grated Parmesan cheese for extra kick of flavor!
