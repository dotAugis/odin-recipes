Pizza Dough

Craving homemade pizza? Start with the perfect dough! This simple recipe uses just a few ingredients and is easy to follow. Once you’ve mastered the dough, you’re ready to create your own pizza masterpiece!

Ingredients:

  • 100ml of water;
  • 200g of type "00" flour;
  • Fresh yeast;
  • A pinch of salt.

Steps:

  1. In a cup, mix warm water & live yeast;
  2. In a big bowl, add flour & salt;
  3. Pour water & live yeast mixture into the flour and salt bowl;
  4. Knead the dough for 4 to 6 minutes.

TIP: If the dough feels too runny, add more flour. If it feels too dry, add tiny bit more water.
