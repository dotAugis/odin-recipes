Pancakes

This classic Italian recipe features a rich, creamy sauce made with eggs, Parmesan cheese, and guanciale (cured pork cheek). The simplicity of the ingredients belies the depth of flavor that can be achieved with this dish. With just a few key steps, you can enjoy a delicious and satisfying meal right at home.

Ingredients:

  • 125g of flour;
  • 2 eggs;
  • 250ml of milk;
  • 2tbsp of sugar;
  • 1tsp of baking powder;
  • 1/4tsp of salt;
  • 1/4tsp of vanilla extract;
  • Butter or oil for greasing the pan.

Steps:

  1. Combine all of the dry ingredients in a bowl and whisk it together;
  2. Combine all of the wet ingredients in a seperate bowl and whisk it together;
  3. Combine the wet and dry ingredients by pouring the wet ingredients into dry;
  4. Stir until combined, but avoid overmixing;
  5. Cook the pancakes on a pre-heated, greased, non-stick pan over medium heat. Pour roughly 1/4 of a cup of batter per pancake and flip after 2 to 3 minutes.

TIP: Serve immediatelly with your favorite toppings, such as vanilla strawberry jam, fruits and even whipped cream to take pancakes to the next level!
